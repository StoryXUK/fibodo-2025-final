Discover The Everything App

Everything you need to know

The Everything App is a wellbeing and lifestyle platform for NHS staff and their families. It offers on-demand and live-streamed content, with discounted local activities and services coming soon.
The app is available to all NHS staff and their immediate family members. Access is granted via NHS email or verification process.
You can enjoy fitness classes, yoga, mindfulness, nutrition, and more—streamed live or available anytime on-demand.
It’s completely free for NHS staff and their families. Some local services may include small charges, but most of the app is free to use.
Live sessions can be joined directly from the app. Check the schedule, set reminders, and access sessions from your phone, tablet, or laptop.
Soon, you’ll be able to book discounted access to gyms, fitness classes, wellness sessions, family activities, and helpful services near you.
Yes! NHS staff can invite their immediate family to use the app and enjoy the content and discounts available.
Most sessions require little to no equipment. If anything specific is needed, it will be mentioned in the class description.
Keep your app notifications turned on, and you’ll receive updates via in-app messages and emails about new content and features.
The Everything App is developed by a team of wellbeing experts and digital innovators, evolving from the Doing Our Bit initiative to better support the NHS community.
