What is Business Marketing Strategy?

A business marketing strategy is a comprehensive plan designed to promote a company’s products or services and achieve its goals. It involves analyzing market trends, understanding customer needs, and positioning the brand effectively. Key elements include defining target audiences, choosing marketing channels, setting objectives, and measuring performance. A well-crafted strategy drives growth and enhances competitive advantage.
